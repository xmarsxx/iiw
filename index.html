Endorsed by History

IIW has been where the identirati meet for the past 15 years. We’ve held over 30 workshops and each has made important strides in online identity.

Community

Hundreds of identity professionals meet at IIW twice a year to connect, discuss, interact, plan, work, and socialize.

Get Work Done

IIW is the home of of important identity protocols like OpenID Connect, OAuth, UMA, and FIDO as well as new technologies like SSI.
